The Characters Of Noli Me Tangere

Maria Clara She is described as a devout Roman Catholic, who became the epitome of virtue. Maria Clara is also the childhood sweetheart and fiance of Juan Crisostomo Ibarra who is the protagonist of the novel.
Crisostomo Ibarra Crisostomo was an earnest and visionary young man. He was motivated to make the country better. He is also engaged to his fiance Maria Clara. He is a son of a wealthy family, despite his status he made sure to be polite to everyone he meets.
Basilio Basilio is a positive and hopeful person even after experiencing hardships in his life. He works very hard so that he could become a successful and good doctor, but he dreamed to be a herdsman so that he could lift his family out of poverty.
Crispin Crispin is the younger brother of Basilio, he studies hard to be a sexton or a caretaker of the church. The mother of Crispin and Basilio is married to a drunk gambler who doesn't provide anything to the family. One night Crispin was going home to visit his mother but the chief sexton interfered saying that he is past curfew and he was beaten merciless and that was when he was last seen.
